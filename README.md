# SuperStore Sales Dashboard

## Project Overview
The SuperStore Sales Dashboard is an interactive Business Intelligence project developed using Power BI to analyze and visualize sales performance across different regions, categories, segments, and payment modes.

This dashboard helps in understanding business trends, identifying profitable categories, tracking monthly sales and profit growth, and evaluating customer purchasing behavior through dynamic visualizations and KPI metrics.

---

## Objectives
- Analyze overall sales and profit performance
- Identify top-performing product categories and sub-categories
- Compare regional sales distribution
- Monitor monthly sales and profit trends
- Evaluate shipping modes and payment methods
- Create an interactive dashboard for business decision-making

---

## Tools & Technologies Used
- Power BI
- DAX (Data Analysis Expressions)
- CSV Dataset
- Data Cleaning & Visualization

---

## Dashboard Features
- Interactive Filters and Slicers
- Sales & Profit KPI Cards
- Regional Sales Analysis
- Category & Sub-Category Insights
- Monthly Sales and Profit Trends
- Payment Mode Analysis
- Ship Mode Performance
- State-wise Sales Visualization

---

## Key Insights
- Consumer segment generated the highest sales contribution.
- Technology category achieved strong revenue performance.
- Online payments accounted for a major share of transactions.
- Western region showed higher sales compared to other regions.
- Monthly sales trends highlighted seasonal business fluctuations.

---

## Dataset Information
The dataset contains SuperStore sales transaction records including:
- Orders
- Sales
- Profit
- Quantity
- Region
- Category
- Sub-category
- Payment mode
- Shipping details

---

## Project Outcome
This dashboard transforms raw sales data into meaningful business insights through interactive visual analytics, helping stakeholders make data-driven decisions efficiently.
